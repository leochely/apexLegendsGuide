Center of the map

The Center Hole

Vey similar to the Hole near Containement but the one is way less exposed. If you manage to be undetected until you reach the respawn beacon, you are safe. You are not visible from any angle and you can easily sound teams that are around because of the area is very narrow. This location is almost the exact center of the map but if you follow the playstyle guidelines, you are not dropping there right away. You will therefore have to go through hot spots like Bridges or East of Bunker. It is perfectly fine to leave one team member behind to delay other teams until you are in position. Before joining the rest of your team in the Center Hole, make sure the ennemies do not have a Bloodhound, as they will immediately know you are there with the Tracker.
